Hockey Schedule

Hockey Schedule is a minimalistic app that shows the full schedule for all 32 teams of the National Hockey League.

A minimalistic app that shows the full schedule for all 32 teams of the National Hockey League (NHL) in a compact layout.

  • Filter out your teams of interest
  • Tap on a game to show details
  • Dark & Light mode
  • Landscape & Portrait mode
