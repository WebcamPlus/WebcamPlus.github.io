Bounce Bonanza

Bounce Bonanza is a 2D bounce ball game where you need to guide the ball through a series of obstacles and make it to the end.

The initial difficulty level is high. You will need to practice your timing and force at which you bounce the ball. Memorizing the map layout may also help you out.

You get points when successfully passing an obstacle. Hitting an obstacle may either result in a points penalty, or game over, depending on the type of obstacle.
