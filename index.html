Webcam+

Use your iPhone as a webcam for your Mac.

Overview

Easy to use and completely free!

  • Resolutions 1920x1080, 1280x720 and 640x480.
  • Frame Rate up to 30 fps.
  • Use any camera on your iPhone.
  • Stream via USB.
  • Control via Menu Bar.

Getting Started

System Requirements

Verify that your setup meets the following requirements:

  • iPhone running iOS 12.4 or later.
  • Mac running macOS 10.13 (High Sierra) or later.
Installation
  1. Download the Webcam+ iOS app.
  2. Download and run the Webcam+ Mac Installer.
  3. Restart the video app that you use your webcam with.
Using the app

With everything installed it is very easy to get started:

  1. Open the Mac app and the iOS app.
  2. Attach the USB cable.
  3. Press Start to start a video session.
  4. You can preview the video by pressing Preview in the Mac app's toolbar.
  5. Select Webcam+ as the camera input in your video app.

Supported Apps

Not all Mac apps support camera plugins. It is the developer of an app that decides whether it should be supported or not. None of Apple's own apps are supported.

The table below lists the app compatibility as of macOS 11.1 Big Sur. This is not a complete list and if the app that you are looking for is not in the list, then feel free to try it out for yourself.
